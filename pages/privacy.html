Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register with Indra Football Academy, we collect:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In or email registration)
  • Age, gender, and experience level
  • Mobile phone number
  • Parent/guardian information (for students under 18)
  • Preferred training location and program selection

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your registration and training enrollment
  • Contact you about schedules, events, and updates
  • Improve our coaching programs and services
  • Communicate via WhatsApp or phone for academy matters

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) with server-side security rules. We use industry-standard encryption and access controls. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google OAuth 2.0).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to IFA administrators for academy management purposes.

5. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • View the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data

Contact us at info.indrafa@gmail.com for any data requests.

6. Cookies

We use Firebase session cookies for authentication purposes only. No third-party tracking cookies are used.
